My steps to greatness.

I am who I am
Now where do I start
See these words are my form of art
Peaceful protestor, black-advocator, warrior,
Poet, big brother, father-figure
But before all of this I was the villian
Chillin in the village
With thoughts thru the ceiling with no fan
No fans making me hot
Being ignored put the villian in a rage
I wanted to be alone cause ifelt like no one innerstood my pain
A distance shadow of my current self in this cold world
Until I met this wonderful Queen
Thinking it was ad distant dream I didn't believe in it
I pushed she stood tall and strong
I screamed she cried
I lied she told the truth
Thinking I was being played by her not realizing I was playing myself
She was my golden ticket out of this hell
Now who am I you aks?
Am I the scared little stuck in the corner mad at the world
The lier, the cold-shoulder
The lover, the fighter, villian or hero
Who am I you ask?
I am who I am that's who I am
So those who know me know me
Those who don't know me get what they see
So believe me if you want
